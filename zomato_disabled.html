How to avail Zomato Pro Membership

1
AURUM Cardholders are required to make their first transaction using their AURUM Card to become eligible for the membership.
2
Apply for Zomato Pro Membership on AURUM website / SBI Card App for AURUM.
3
Once activated, AURUM Cardholder receives the Zomato Pro Membership ID with a validity of 1 Year from the date the membership is activated.
4
For existing Zomato Pro members, the membership gets extended by 1 year
5
In case the AURUM Cardholder is an existing Zomato Pro member with an Unlock pack/ Weekday plan, etc. then the membership would be upgraded to an Annual all India membership.
6
Membership ID and Validity will be visible on AURUM Website, SBI Card App for AURUM
