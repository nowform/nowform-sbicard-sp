How to avail Discovery Plus Membership

1
AURUM Cardholders are required to make their first transaction using their AURUM Card to become eligible for the membership.
2
Eligible AURUM customers will receive E-code via SMS which needs to be applied in Discovery Plus app.
3
Android users:

  • Step 1: Install and open the Discovery Plus App.
  • Step 2: Click on top left of the App homepage to open the main menu
  • Step 3: Click on ‘Redeem Voucher’ button
  • Step 4: Login/Register
  • Step 5: Enter the E-code and click on continue to avail the premium subscription
4
IOS users:

  • Step 1: Go to www.discoveryplus.in/premium and click on continue
  • Step 2: Login/Register
  • Step 3: Select annual INR 299 plan and click on subscribe
  • Step 4: Enter E- code and click on continue to avail the premium subscription
