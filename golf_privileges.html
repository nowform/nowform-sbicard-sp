Golf Privileges

Tee off with AURUM to enjoy an indulgent golfing experience

What's in store for members

4 complimentary golf visits & 12 complimentary golf lessons in a year
50% discount on subsequent visits (capped to one round per month)
I agree to the terms and conditions of AURUM Golf Privileges.
You will be redirected to an external portal.

How to avail AURUM Golf Privileges

1
First Time Customer – The Customer will register itself on teepassindia.thriwe.com by clicking on the Sign up option and provide the following details:
  1. First & Last Name
  2. Mobile Number
  3. Email Address
  4. First 6 digits of the card
After providing these details, the account will have to get activated by doing a pre-authorization of INR 1, which will be refunded back to the customer within 24 hours of the transaction.
2
Once the user has registered on the website and created the password, user will get the username on his registered email. Customer can then login to view his golf benefit details. In order to place a booking, customer will have to click on ‘Book Your Golf“ button.
3
After clicking on to the “Book Your Golf ” button select the golf course, date and time of play basis the offered program. Also, under the comment section the customer can mention the name of the golf club member or the guest the customer wants to play with.
4
On the last step of placing a complimentary booking, the customer will have to make a transaction of INR 1 or a pre-auth of INR 1.
5
Once the customer submits the request, an email will go to his/ her registered email address. This email will be treated as the booking acknowledgement email.
