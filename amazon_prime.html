How to avail Amazon Prime Membership

1
Make your first transaction using your AURUM Card
2
The cardholder shall receive an e-link on her registered mobile number within 2 weeks of first transaction
3
This e-link is valid for usage within 6 months from the date this e-link is sent
4
On clicking this e-link, the customer is directed to the Amazon site where customer can log in to their amazon account or sign up for a new account
5
On the landing page, customer enters payment details. Message will be shown about refund policy
6
Rs 2 is charged to verify the account, membership auto renews to an annual plan on the payment instrument entered by the customer at the end of 12 months.
7
12-month prime membership starts for the customer from that very day.
8
Auto-renew can be turned off and the Prime membership will discontinue at the end of the promo period
